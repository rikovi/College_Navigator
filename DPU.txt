 
				Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Pune
- OVERVIEW
    Institute name: Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth
    Also known as: DPU
    Institute Type: Private
    Established: 2002
    Location: Pune, Maharashtra
- CONNECTIVITY:
    Pune International Airport: 25 km
    Pune Railway Station:       15 km
- MODE OF ADMISSION
  Admission to DPU College is primarily based on entrance examinations and merit. The college conducts its entrance tests for specific courses, and admission criteria vary depending on the program.
- FEE STRUCTURE
  DPU College offers competitive fee structures for its courses. It also provides scholarships and financial assistance to deserving students based on merit and need.
- AFFILIATIONS
  Affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU). It is “Deemed-to-be-University” under Section 3 of the UGC Act 1956.
- ON-CAMPUS COMPANIES
  Some of the notable recruiters include Tata Motors, Reliance Industries, Infosys, and HDFC Bank.
