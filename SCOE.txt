                                  
                              Sinhgad College of Engineering (SCOE)
- OVERVIEW
    Institute Nmae: Sinhgad College of Engineering 
    Also known as: SCOE
    Institute Type: Private
    Established: 1996
    Location: Pune, Maharashtra
- CONNECTIVITY
    Pune International Airport: 20 km
    Pune Railway Station:       10 km
- MODE OF ADMISSION
  Admission to Sinhagad College is primarily based on entrance examinations and merit. The college conducts its entrance tests for specific courses, and admission criteria vary depending on the program.
- FEE STRUCTURE
  Sinhagad College offers competitive fee structures for its courses. It also provides scholarships and financial assistance to deserving students based on merit and need.
- AFFILIATIONS
  The University of Pune.
- ON-CAMPUS COMPANIES
  Some prominent recruiters include Amazon, Capgemini, Infosys, and Bajaj Auto.
